about us...

Our journey began in early 1963 in alwarpet,chennai and grew to over one thousand location throughout india with the love of our customers.Creating exceptional cakes is our way of life, and our customers and partners are the focus of everything that we do. All our products and services shall be offered with the same love, care, and affection as if, they were meant for the most beloved person.We shall make products keeping in mind the feelings of the end consumer and make the products with the same love, care and affection as if it were made for a family member. golden bears believes in order to give the Real taste of any product, real ingredients should be used & not cheaper substitutes. Our production includes a highly-customized, automated system but does not forego the importance of the human touch, while still adhering to strict quality control measures at every stage of production, packaging, and delivery. Combining technology and people is what ensures that our products are the best. Without our dedicated, skilled, and loyal team members,golden bears would not be where it is today.
